## About

**Alor Uttaradhikar – A Legacy of Light** is a Bengali cinematic audio documentary drama dedicated to the 125th Birth Anniversary of Dr. Shyama Prasad Mukherjee.

The project combines:

- Historical narrative
- Educational storytelling
- Radio drama
- Poetry
- Music
- Sound design
- Voice acting
- Stage adaptation
